## 4 · THE HUFFLE — PART 1: OVERVIEW *(Angela voice log — Sound Recorder)*
- ▶ VOICE LOG · DR. ANGELA WARD · HRUMF ACADEMY RESEARCH CENTER · JUNE 2022 · PART 1/2
- *"For all their differences, every Huffle shares one defining trait — their emotional state directly drives their biology."*
- **Who they are** — Adorably odd, clumsy, fiercely loyal, deeply emotional; driven to end sadness and to *fit in*. It rarely ends well.
- **How they talk** — No human language: snorts, snuffles, sighs, giggles, wild theatrical gestures. (A rare few adopt one word and repeat it forever.)
- **What they look like** — Roughly human-shaped: bipedal, round, stubby limbs, big eyes. Past that, no two alike.
- **The impossible part** — Essentially immortal: no ageing, no pain, no injury, no need to eat or drink.
- **The catch** — Their biology runs on **emotion** — and an unhappy Huffle starts doing very strange things…

## 5 · THE HUFFLE — PART 2: BIOLOGY + BEHAVIOUR *(process visuals)*
- ▶ VOICE LOG · DR. ANGELA WARD · PART 2/2 · *"A Huffle's mood is to its body what oxygen is to ours. Under prolonged unhappiness, it defends itself — by escaping, or by making friends."*
- **PROCESSES:**
  - **Inflation** — unhappy → swells → drifts away on the breeze. An involuntary escape.
  - **Duplication** *(the engine)* — **Sad → multiply.** Alone, it cocoons; 48 hrs later it splits into several. It grows its own friends.
  - **Fusing** *(the engine)* — **Happy → merge.** Two embrace, cocoon in a marshmallow shell, emerge as one larger Huffle.
  - **Parapsychology** — telepathy · telekinesis · teleportation. Sworn worldwide; unproven.
- **BEHAVIOURS:** Consoling (comforts anything sad) · Bonding (fixates on one object) · Strutting (shows off its "human-ness") · Nesting (hidden dens from found scraps).

## 6 · THE KIDS WHO SAVED THE WORLD *(origin)*
- **The world's governments couldn't fix it. A bunch of kids did.**
- By 2010 the population was doubling and doubling again. Adults panicked — caged them, ran — and every frightened Huffle made two more. The crisis fed itself.
- The answer came from **Madeira, Ohio** — kids who rode out to *help* instead of hide: **Happy Huffles don't multiply.**
- They wrote the *Huffle Helpers Handbook* by hand and put it online. Within a year it crossed the planet. The curve bent. The crisis broke.
- A twelve-year-old named **Cindy Sloane** led that pack. The watch she started has a name now, a building, and a budget in the billions. It's called **HRUMF.**
- Timeline: 2000 THE HAPPENING · 2010 MADEIRA CRACKS IT · 2025 25 YEARS ON

## 13 · THE SCHISM
- Two kids started the Huffle Helpers on the same street in Madeira. Cindy Sloane was one. Her best friend — maybe more — was **Chris Tanti**. They drew the Helpers' first logo together.
- Then a Huffle took everything from him. A terrified stowaway in the engine room of his parents' anniversary cruise. It ballooned. The ship went down — and his parents with it. Killed by the gentlest creature on Earth, because it was *scared.*
- Cindy called it a tragedy. Chris called it a *verdict.* They were eighteen. They never spoke again.
- **Fourteen years later, Cindy runs HRUMF. And Chris runs the thing trying to destroy it.**
