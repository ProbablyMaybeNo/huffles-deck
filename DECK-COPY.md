## 4 · WHAT IS A HUFFLE (biology)
- `HRUMF · SEEKER DIVISION · PUBLIC BIOLOGICAL BRIEF 04-A`
- After 25 years of study, the honest truth: **we still don't know where Huffles come from, or why they're here.** What we *have* proven is stranger.
- ▪ Indestructible — never cut, pierced, or injured. Ever. · ▪ Ageless — none recorded dying of old age. · ▪ Doesn't eat — feeds on belonging, kindness, being part of something. · ▪ Emotional body — runs on feelings. Visibly.
- CORE MECHANIC: loved = calm; alone = not. MULTIPLY: 1 sad → 2. MERGE: 2 happy → 1.
- **A world that mistreats its Huffles makes *more* Huffles.** That's the engine the whole universe runs on.

## 5 · THE KIDS WHO SAVED THE WORLD (origin)
- **The world's governments couldn't fix it. A bunch of kids did.**
- By 2010 the population was doubling and doubling again. Adults panicked — caged them, ran — and every frightened Huffle made two more. The crisis fed itself.
- The answer came from **Madeira, Ohio** — kids who rode out to *help* instead of hide. They figured out what every expert missed: **Happy Huffles don't multiply.**
- They wrote it by hand — the *Huffle Helpers Handbook* — and put it online. Within a year it crossed the planet. The curve bent. The crisis broke.
- A twelve-year-old named **Cindy Sloane** led that pack. The watch she started has a name now, a building, and a budget in the billions. It's called **HRUMF.**
- Timeline: 2000 THE HAPPENING · 2010 MADEIRA CRACKS IT · 2025 25 YEARS ON

## 12 · THE SCHISM
- Two kids started the Huffle Helpers on the same street in Madeira. Cindy Sloane was one. Her best friend — maybe more — was **Chris Tanti**. They drew the Helpers' first logo together.
- Then a Huffle took everything from him. A terrified stowaway in the engine room of his parents' anniversary cruise. It ballooned. The ship went down — and his parents with it. Killed by the gentlest creature on Earth, because it was *scared.*
- Cindy called it a tragedy. Chris called it a *verdict.* They were eighteen. They never spoke again.
- **Fourteen years later, Cindy runs HRUMF. And Chris runs the thing trying to destroy it.**
